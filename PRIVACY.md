# Privacy Policy for Songs

**Last Updated:** [Date]  *(e.g., June 13, 2026)*

This privacy policy describes how the "Songs" music player ("we", "us", or "our") handles your information.

We take your privacy seriously. This policy outlines the types of information we do and do not collect when you use our service.

## Information We Collect
**We do not collect, store, or share any personal information.** This application is a static, client-side web app. It does not use a backend server, databases, or any form of persistent storage that can record your data.

## How the App Uses the YouTube API
This app uses the YouTube API to search for and play music videos. Any data exchanged with the YouTube API is handled directly between your browser and YouTube. We act only as a bridge, and we do not intercept, log, or store any of this data.

Your use of YouTube's services is subject to their own policies. We encourage you to review them:
*   [YouTube Terms of Service](https://www.youtube.com/t/terms)
*   [Google Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy)

## Your Data, Your Control
Because we do not collect any user data, there is no information for you to access, modify, or delete from our system.

If you have concerns about data that may be stored by the YouTube API, please refer to your Google Account settings or contact Google directly, as outlined in their privacy policy.

## Changes to This Privacy Policy
We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date.
